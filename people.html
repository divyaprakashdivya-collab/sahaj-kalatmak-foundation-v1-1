People Behind Sahaj

Vision becomes reality through the people who carry it forward. Meet the individuals shaping Sahaj Kalatmak Foundation with leadership, cultural sensitivity, creative planning, mentorship, and thoughtful guidance.

FOUNDER / DIRECTOR

Arun Shekhar

Cultural Vision & Institutional Leadership

Arun Shekhar provides the founding vision and cultural direction of Sahaj Kalatmak Foundation. His leadership reflects a belief in art as a meaningful medium of dialogue, sensitivity, and social connection.

CO-DIRECTOR

Shaurya Singh

Youth Leadership & Public Engagement

Shaurya Singh strengthens the foundation through young leadership, public engagement, and contemporary cultural perspective. Her role supports Sahaj’s evolving connection with newer audiences and creative communities.

PROGRAM MANAGER

Divya Prakash

Creative Strategy & Program Design

Divya Prakash contributes through program planning, creative communication, content design, and event coordination. He helps transform cultural ideas into structured initiatives and meaningful audience experiences.

CULTURAL MENTOR

Jagdish Nishad

Community Outreach & Cultural Support

Jagdish Nishad brings cultural understanding, community connection, and supportive mentorship to the foundation’s activities. His presence adds warmth, experience, and grounded participation to Sahaj’s cultural work.

SENIOR ADVISOR

Sriprakash Menon

Guidance, Wisdom & Strategic Support

Sriprakash Menon offers mature guidance, thoughtful perspective, and strategic support to the foundation. His advisory presence strengthens Sahaj’s long-term cultural direction and institutional credibility.
